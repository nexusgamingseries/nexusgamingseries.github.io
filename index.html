For five seasons, the Nexus Gaming Series (NGS) has been bringing Heroes of the Storm players of all skill levels together in the desire for a balanced, competitive experience. We're a close-knit community of people who all have a single goal: having fun! To that end, we're defined by our motto: "By the players, for the players". From Bronze to Grand Masters, our community is diverse in its levels of play, and we pride ourselves on being ready, willing, and able to provide learning opportunities in the process of having fair and compelling tournament-style matches. Sign up, show your skills, set up some scrims, and join us in the Nexus for some good old-fashioned combat! Come on in, the water's fine.
